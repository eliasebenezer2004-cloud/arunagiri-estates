## 2) Brand Story & Business Personality

**15. How did your real estate journey begin?**
I started as a property consultant helping my father's friends buy land in Kanchipuram and Chengalpattu in 2008-09. From there I slowly built a network of property owners and buyers in Chennai.

**16. What inspired you to enter this market?**
Tamil Nadu — especially Chennai — has always been a high-demand real estate market. IT growth, educational institutions, and cultural significance make it a forever market. I saw that most agents lacked professionalism and transparency, and I wanted to build trust.

**17. Story or meaning behind the company name:**
"Arunagiri" comes from my name Arun and the Tamil word "giri" (mountain/hill) — symbolizing stability, strength, and something that lasts. It also resonates with Arunagiri, the Tamil saint-poet, giving it a cultural touch.

**18. What makes your business different?**
We speak the client's language — literally and figuratively. We deal in Tamil and English. We don't push properties; we understand what the family needs first. Our after-sale support is strong — we help with registration, loan facilitation, and even finding tenants for investors. We are not a high-volume, low-service agency.

**19. What are you most proud of?**
Successfully closing over 350+ property transactions since 2009, and the fact that 40% of our clients come through referrals. Also, completing a large villa project in OMR where we helped 45 families find their dream homes.

**20. Core values:**
Transparency in every deal, respect for the client's budget, patience in explaining the process, complete documentation support, and treating every client like family.

**21. What should visitors understand immediately?**
That we are a trustworthy, local, experienced real estate firm — not a flashy broker. We care about finding the right property, not just closing any deal.

**22. Feeling or impression after visiting the website?**
Confidence. The visitor should feel "This team knows what they are doing, and I can trust them with my money and my family's future."

**23. Three brand words:**
Trustworthy, Grounded, Responsive.

**24. Reputation in the next 5 years:**
Become the most trusted mid-size real estate consultancy in Chennai and Tamil Nadu — known for ethical deals, excellent customer service, and deep local market knowledge.

**25. Personal stories, struggles, or milestones?**
In 2012, I almost lost a client's advance because of a property title issue. That experience taught me to verify every document twice. We now have a 3-step title verification process. Also, during COVID-19 (2020-21), we survived by focusing on resale properties and rental assistance — that period taught us to diversify.

**26. Personal brand, company brand, or both?**
Both. The company brand (Arunagiri Estates) should be primary, but my personal credibility and story should support it — especially on the About page and in trust-building sections.

## Additional Notes & Requirements

### Multilingual Support
We want the website to be available in both **Tamil and English** with a language toggle. A significant portion of our clients are more comfortable in Tamil. The Tamil content should be professionally translated, not Google-translated.

### Mobile-First Design
Over 80% of our traffic comes from mobile phones. The website MUST be fully responsive and mobile-optimized. Click-to-call and WhatsApp buttons should be thumb-friendly on mobile.

### Speed & Performance
Our current website is slow. The new site must score 90+ on Google PageSpeed. Optimize images, use lazy loading, and choose a fast hosting setup.

### SEO Requirements
We want to rank for keywords like:
- "real estate consultant in Chennai"
- "flats for sale in OMR"
- "plots for sale in Chengalpattu"
- "property for sale in Adyar"
- "NRI property investment Chennai"
- "homes for sale in Velachery"
- "real estate agent near me Chennai"

We need proper on-page SEO, schema markup for properties, and a blog section for content marketing.

### Blog / Content Section
We want a blog section where we can post:
- Area guides (e.g., "Complete Guide to Buying Flats in OMR")
- Market updates (e.g., "Chennai Real Estate Market Trends 2026")
- Tips for buyers (e.g., "10 Things to Check Before Buying a Resale Flat")
- Registration and legal process explainers

This is important for SEO and establishing authority.

### Google Business Profile Integration
We want the website to integrate with our Google Business Profile. We also want an embedded Google Map on the contact page and property detail pages.

### Lead Capture & CRM
We need a simple lead capture system — when someone submits an inquiry form, we want:
1. An auto-reply email/WhatsApp message to the lead
2. A notification to our phone (SMS or WhatsApp notification)
3. Lead data stored in a simple dashboard we can access

We do not need a complex CRM — a simple WordPress plugin or Google Sheets integration would work.

### Social Media Integration
Links to our Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube pages. We want social media icons in the header and footer. We also want Open Graph tags so our property pages look good when shared on WhatsApp/social media.

### WhatsApp Integration
A floating WhatsApp chat button on all pages. It should open a pre-filled message like: "Hi Arunagiri Estates, I'm interested in [property name/URL]. Please share details."

### Security
SSL certificate, secure forms, spam protection on contact forms, and regular backups.

### Budget & Timeline
We are comfortable investing in a quality website. Our budget is ₹1.5L – ₹2.5L for design and development. We want the website launched within 6-8 weeks.
