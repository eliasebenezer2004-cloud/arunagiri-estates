5 Tips for First-Time Home Buyers in Chennai

Essential guidance for navigating Chennai's property market with confidence.

May 15, 2026 · Arunagiri Estates · Buying

Buying your first home is one of the most significant financial decisions you will ever make. In a dynamic market like Chennai, where property values have been steadily appreciating across corridors like OMR, Velachery, and Porur, making an informed choice is critical. At Arunagiri Estates & Homes, we have helped hundreds of first-time buyers navigate this journey, and we have distilled our experience into five essential tips.

1. Define Your Budget Before You Start Looking

Chennai offers a wide range of properties — from affordable 2BHK apartments in developing suburbs to premium villas in established neighbourhoods. Before you begin your search, get pre-approved for a home loan to understand exactly what you can afford. Factor in additional costs such as stamp duty (7% in Tamil Nadu), registration fees, GST for under-construction properties, and maintenance deposits. A clear budget will help you narrow your options and avoid the disappointment of falling in love with a property outside your range.

2. Research the Locality Thoroughly

The neighbourhood you choose will define your daily life and the long-term value of your investment. Check for proximity to workplaces, schools, hospitals, public transport, and daily amenities. In Chennai, areas near Metro stations and IT corridors have seen the highest appreciation. Visit the locality at different times of the day to assess traffic, noise levels, and the general vibe. Talk to residents and local shopkeepers to get an unfiltered perspective on the area.

3. Verify Legal Titles and RERA Registration

One of the most common pitfalls for first-time buyers is neglecting legal due diligence. Ensure the property has clear title deeds with no encumbrances. Verify that the project is registered under RERA (Real Estate Regulation Act) — this guarantees that the builder has disclosed project timelines, approvals, and layout plans. Our team at Arunagiri Estates provides thorough legal verification services to give you complete peace of mind before you commit.

4. Compare Under-Construction vs Ready-to-Move Options

Under-construction properties typically come at a lower price point and offer flexible payment plans, but they carry the risk of construction delays and require patience. Ready-to-move homes allow you to inspect the actual unit and move in immediately, but they usually command a premium. If you are on a tight timeline or prefer certainty, a ready-to-move property is the safer bet. If you can wait 2-3 years, an under-construction project from a reputed builder can yield better returns.

5. Work with a Trusted Real Estate Partner

Navigating Chennai's property market on your own can be overwhelming. A trusted real estate agent brings local market knowledge, access to off-market listings, negotiation expertise, and end-to-end documentation support. At Arunagiri Estates & Homes, we prioritise transparency and client satisfaction above all. Whether you are looking for a budget-friendly apartment or a luxury villa, we are here to make your first home purchase a smooth and rewarding experience.
